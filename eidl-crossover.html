Multiple Loans in One Case

If you have both an EIDL and a 7(a) or 504 loan, all can be addressed in a single filing. Each is listed as a separate claim. Discharge covers all personal guarantees.

Coordinating Treatment

Each loan's lien is analyzed separately. The SBA may hold EIDL directly while 7(a) involves a private lender. List each creditor separately.

Strategic Considerations

Subchapter V handles all business and personal debts in one case. If the business is closed, Chapter 7 eliminates all guarantees in ~4 months.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does multiple SBA debt affect eligibility?

No limit on amount to discharge. For Ch.13, debts must be under $2.75M. For Subchapter V, under $7.5M.

Can the SBA offset my refund while I'm in bankruptcy?

No. The automatic stay prevents all offsets for all debts listed in the petition.

What about PPP loans?

Forgiven PPP is not a debt. Unforgiven PPP converted to SBA loans is treated the same as any other SBA loan.
