Home » Credit Rebuilding After SBA Bankruptcy

Immediate Steps

Check credit reports, dispute inaccuracies, get a secured credit card, keep non-discharged obligations current. Rebuilding starts the day of discharge.

Recovery Timeline

Improvement within 6-12 months. Unsecured credit in 12-24 months. Auto loans in 12-18 months. FHA mortgage in 2 years after Ch.7 or 1 year into Ch.13.

Starting a New Business

Bankruptcy does not prevent new businesses. Form new entities, get EIN, open accounts. SBA loans unavailable initially but alternative financing can fill the gap. Many successful entrepreneurs have bankruptcy in their past.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get another SBA loan?

Not immediately. Generally need 2-3 years of positive credit history and 680+ score. Prior loans must be resolved (discharge counts).

Will vendors work with me?

Most will. Business relationships are based on current behavior. Offer cash or prepay initially to build trust.

How do I explain the bankruptcy?

Be direct. Explain circumstances and what changed. Investors care more about your plan and integrity than credit history.
