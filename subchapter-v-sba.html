Why Subchapter V Is Ideal

Debtor stays in control, no creditors' committee, no disclosure statement, and the plan can be confirmed over SBA objections. Restructure debt and keep the business alive.

Plan Requirements

Pay projected disposable income for 3-5 years. Not discriminate unfairly. Be fair and equitable. Be feasible. The plan can modify SBA loan terms.

SBA Loan Modification

Extend maturity, reduce interest rate to market rate, strip lien to collateral value, or surrender collateral and discharge the deficiency.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Subchapter V cost?

Attorney fees $15,000-$30,000. Filing fee $1,738. The trustee is paid from plan payments.

Can sole proprietors use Subchapter V?

Yes. The plan addresses both business and personal debts in one case.

What if the plan fails?

Convert to Chapter 7 or dismiss. Having a realistic plan is critical.
